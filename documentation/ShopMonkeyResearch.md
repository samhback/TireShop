# ShopMonkey Research

## Overview

ShopMonkey is a cloud-based shop management platform built for auto repair and tire shops. Below are the features a rural Oklahoma tire shop would realistically use day-to-day.

---

## Features a Tire Shop Would Actually Use

### 1. Appointment Scheduling
- Book and manage customer appointments
- Avoid overbooking with calendar management
- Automatically send appointment confirmations and reminders via text/email

### 2. Estimates & Quotes
- Quickly build tire quotes (by size, brand, service type)
- Pre-built service packages (e.g. rotation + balance + alignment bundles)
- Send estimates to customers digitally for approval before work begins

### 3. Invoicing & Payments
- Generate and send invoices via text or email
- Accept credit cards, chip cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay
- Track paid vs. unpaid invoices

### 4. Inventory Management
- Track tires and parts separately by size, brand, and quantity
- Get low-stock alerts
- Order tires directly through integrations with suppliers (TPI, ATD)

### 5. Customer Management (CRM)
- Store customer vehicle history and contact info
- Send automated follow-up reminders (e.g. "time to rotate your tires")
- Manage online reputation / reviews

### 6. Digital Vehicle Inspections
- Technicians can log inspection notes and photos digitally
- Share inspection results with customers before approving work

### 7. Reporting & Analytics
- Track shop performance metrics (revenue, car count, tech efficiency)
- Payment summaries and profitability reports
- Helps owners make data-driven decisions

### 8. Technician Management
- Assign jobs to specific techs
- Track technician efficiency and workload

---

## Notes for Our App

A rural Oklahoma tire shop likely won't need every enterprise feature ShopMonkey offers.
The **core MVP** for Logan's shop probably comes down to:

- Appointments
- Estimates / Invoices
- Basic inventory tracking
- Customer records & vehicle history
- Payment collection
- Sales reports

Start there. Keep it simple.
