# Logan Requirements

## Shop Details

- **Shop name:** Healdton Service Center
- **Owner:** Logan
- **Source:** Direct conversation with Logan

## Required Capabilities

The app needs to support these core business workflows:

- Inventory tracking
- Sales reports
- Part numbers
- Pricing
- Quantities on hand
- Customer information
- Vehicle information
- Maintenance logs
- Invoice creation
- Receiving and recording payments for invoices

## MVP Interpretation

For the first usable version, Healdton Service Center needs a practical shop management system that lets Logan:

- Log in to an internal dashboard
- Look up inventory by tire, part, or part number
- See pricing and quantity on hand before quoting or invoicing a job
- Create invoices for completed work
- Record cash/card/manual payments against invoices
- Know which invoices are unpaid or partially paid
- Pull sales totals for a selected date range
- Open a customer profile and see vehicles, mileage, maintenance history, invoices, and payments

## Not Current Focus

Do not prioritize customer-facing pages yet. Public marketing pages, online booking, customer invoice portals, text/email messaging, and online payment links can come later after the internal workflow is useful.
